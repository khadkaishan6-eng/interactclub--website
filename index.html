About Our Club

The Interact Club of Little Angels' School is a community of young leaders dedicated to serving others and making a positive impact in our world. As part of Rotary International, we follow the motto "Service Above Self".

Our club organizes various service projects, community events, and fundraising activities throughout the year. We believe in developing leadership skills while making a difference in our community.
